My Protest

Chicago Young Feminists Meetup

Location: Town Hall, Chicago, IL
Date: 03/08/2016, 1:00 PM
Tag: #feminist, #Chicago, #Illinois
Description:

Chicago Young Feminism is for the meditating woman (and man) who seeks a fast and powerful path to the truth of female power, productivity and fulfillment. It is for the woman who wants rapid, transformative realizations about women, men and self realization. Since most of what we have been taught and observe about being a man or a woman is inaccurate, discussions will uncover new insights as to the how, why and what of the present situation as well as present a truthful, individual look at the future based on the spiritual nature of men and women. Meetings will be interactive and fun.
